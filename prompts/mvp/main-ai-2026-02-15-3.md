# Prompt: main-ai-2026-02-15-3

## Input
I am a receptionist at a dental office that is using the same software. What kind of things am I inputing into the application

## Output

As a receptionist at a dental office, your inputs are primarily administrative and patient-facing:

**Patient Registration & Demographics**
- New patient intake (name, DOB, address, phone, email)
- Emergency contact information
- Responsible party / guarantor details
- Patient photo and ID scan

**Insurance & Billing**
- Insurance carrier, group number, subscriber info
- Verifying and entering eligibility/benefit details
- Copay and payment collection (cash, card, check)
- Processing payment plans and financing (e.g., CareCredit)
- Submitting and tracking insurance claims
- Posting insurance payments and adjustments
- Generating patient statements and invoices

**Scheduling**
- Booking, rescheduling, and canceling appointments
- Operatory and provider assignment
- Appointment type and duration
- Recall/recare scheduling (6-month cleanings, etc.)
- Managing the waitlist and filling cancellations
- Blocking provider time off and lunch breaks

**Communication & Follow-Up**
- Appointment confirmations and reminders (text, email, phone)
- Logging phone calls and messages
- Referral tracking (incoming and outgoing)
- Patient correspondence and letters

**Check-In / Check-Out**
- Marking patients as arrived/checked in
- Updating medical history and consent forms (often digital)
- Collecting and scanning signed documents
- Checking patients out and scheduling follow-ups

**Administrative Records**
- Updating patient contact info and preferences
- Notes on patient communication preferences (language, preferred contact method)
- Tracking incomplete treatment and follow-up needed
- Managing new patient paperwork and document scanning
