# Prompt: main-ai-2026-02-15-1

## Input
I am working at a dentist office as a dental hygienist and use software on my desktop. What kind of things am I inputing into the application

## Output

As a dental hygienist, you'd typically input the following into dental practice management software:

**Patient Clinical Data**
- Periodontal charting (pocket depths, bleeding on probing, recession)
- Existing and missing teeth
- Oral cancer screening findings
- Hard/soft tissue assessments

**Treatment Documentation**
- Procedures performed (prophylaxis, scaling & root planing, fluoride, sealants)
- ADA procedure codes (D-codes) for billing
- Teeth/quadrants treated
- Anesthesia administered (type, amount, location)

**Radiographs**
- X-ray images (bitewings, periapicals, panos)
- Radiographic findings and notes

**Patient Health Information**
- Medical history updates (medications, allergies, conditions)
- Blood pressure and vitals
- Health questionnaire responses

**Progress Notes**
- Clinical narratives for each visit
- Patient complaints or concerns
- Home care instructions given
- Recommendations for follow-up treatment

**Scheduling & Administrative**
- Recare/recall intervals
- Next appointment notes
- Referrals to specialists

**Assessments & Indices**
- Plaque scores
- Gingival indices
- Risk assessments (caries, periodontal)

The core of the work revolves around periodontal charting and treatment documentation, which drive both the clinical record and the billing side.
